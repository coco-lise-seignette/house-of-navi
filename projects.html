№ 01 Healthcare

Mkasanga Community Clinic

South Luangwa · Zambia

A community built clinic on the edge of the wild. Mkasanga sits six to eight hours from the nearest hospital, with roads often cut off during the rains. Pregnant mothers, children, and families currently travel extreme distances for basic care. The clinic is already halfway built by the local community. The mission now is to help complete it.
