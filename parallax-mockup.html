ABOUT

We arrive with a spotlight, not a solution.

THE NAVI PROMISE

House of Navi is a South African non-profit shining a light on carefully curated local projects across the continent — and connecting them to the donors, storytellers and changemakers who can help carry them forward.

We don't lead with statistics. We lead with the people on the ground, and the work already in motion, and we let the rest of the world catch up. Stories first. Funding follows.

Conservation and community are not separate conversations. They are the same one — and we will keep listening for as long as it takes.

— the House of Navi team

Founded 2024 · Cape Town, South Africa
